Wagyu Steak

Description

Cooked Wagyu steak is a premium beef dish known for its exceptional tenderness, marbling, and rich, buttery flavor. It is often prepared by searing it briefly on high heat to achieve a caramelized crust while maintaining a rare to medium-rare interior. Wagyu steak is celebrated for its melt-in-your-mouth texture and exquisite taste, making it a luxurious dining experience.

Ingredients

  • Wagyu steak
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Olive oil or clarified butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the steak by taking it out of the fridge
  2. Season the steak
  3. Preheat the pan
  4. Sear the steak
  5. Let the steak rest on a cutting board
