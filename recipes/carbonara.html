Pasta Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish renowned for its simplicity and rich, creamy flavor. It typically consists of spaghetti or another pasta variety tossed with a sauce made from eggs, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, guanciale (cured pork jowl), and black pepper. The eggs create a creamy texture when combined with the hot pasta, and the cheese adds a salty, savory depth of flavor. It's a delicious and satisfying dish that showcases the beauty of Italian culinary minimalism.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or pasta of choice
  • Eggs
  • Eggs
  • Grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Guanciale or pancetta (cured pork jowl or belly)
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare the sauce ingredients
  3. Cook the Guanciale or Pancetta
  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  5. Toss Pasta with Sauce
  6. Add Egg and Cheese Mixture
  7. Adjust Consistency
  8. Serve Immediately
