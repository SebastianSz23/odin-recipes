Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made by layering flat lasagna noodles with a savory combination of ingredients, including rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, ground meat (such as beef, pork, or sausage), and a generous amount of melted cheese. It's a hearty and comforting dish often enjoyed in square or rectangular slices.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Boil lasgna noodles
  2. Prepare Tomato Sauce
  3. Make Béchamel Sauce
  4. Layer the Lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Rest and Serve
